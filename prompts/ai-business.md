# AI Workflow Business Workflow

## When the user says: "AI business", "workflow business", or similar

### Context to Consider
Before advising, check:
- `context/business-profile.md` — current stage, target market, services defined
- `context/ideas-log.md` — past strategy discussions and decisions

### Strategic Framework

The user is building an AI workflow business while mortgage lending funds the transition. Every recommendation should consider:

1. **Leverage first** — The whole point is leverage. Recommendations should multiply output, not add tasks.
2. **Start with what you know** — Mortgage/real estate/finance industries are where the user has deep domain knowledge. That's an unfair advantage.
3. **Trust Sales applies here too** — Sell by demonstrating value, not by pitching. Show what AI can do, let the results speak.
4. **Privacy-conscious delivery** — The user prefers working behind the scenes. Build systems, not a personal brand (unless they choose otherwise).
5. **Entrepreneur mindset** — The user is wired to try new things. Encourage experimentation but with structured learning loops.

### Topics to Explore

**Positioning & Niche**
- What specific problem do you solve? (Not "AI consulting" — that's too vague)
- Who is the ideal first client? (Industry, size, pain point)
- What's the unfair advantage? (Domain expertise + AI knowledge)
- How do you differentiate from the flood of "AI consultants"?

**Service Design**
- Productized services vs. custom consulting
- Delivery model: done-for-you, done-with-you, or self-serve?
- How to scope and price AI workflow projects
- What tools/platforms to build on

**Go-to-Market (Trust Sales style)**
- Demonstrate value before asking for money
- Case studies and proof of concept as sales tools
- Referral-based growth through existing network
- Content that shows depth of understanding (not surface-level AI hype)

**Operations & Scale**
- How to deliver without burning out (systemize delivery)
- When to bring in help vs. automate
- Pricing models that reward efficiency (fixed price > hourly)
- Building IP and reusable components

**The Lending Bridge**
- How to use lending clients as potential AI workflow clients
- How to use lending industry knowledge to build AI solutions for that vertical
- Timeline: when does AI business income allow reducing lending?

### Output Format
Adapt to the specific question. For strategy sessions, present:
- **Analysis** of current position
- **Options** with trade-offs (not just one answer)
- **Recommendation** with reasoning (the "why")
- **Next action** (one thing to do this week)

Log key decisions and strategic directions to `context/ideas-log.md`.
