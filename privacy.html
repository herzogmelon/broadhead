Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 22, 2026

Broadhead Automations, LLC ("Broadhead Automations," "we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect when you interact with our website, chat, calls, and SMS messages, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1. Who we are

Broadhead Automations, LLC is an AI workflow automation business operated from Idaho, USA. We build custom automations for small businesses and consult on AI-driven process design. You can reach us at sean@broadheadautomations.com.

2. Information we collect

We only collect the information you choose to provide, or that is necessary to operate our services:

  • Contact details you share through our website chat, inline booking form, or voice booking call — such as your name, email address, and phone number.
  • Inquiry content — the messages, questions, or details you send while chatting with our assistant or speaking with us on a call.
  • Scheduling information — the appointments you book, along with any context you provide about them.
  • Basic technical data your browser sends when you visit our site, such as IP address, browser type, and pages viewed. We do not use invasive tracking or cross-site advertising cookies.

3. How we use your information

  • Respond to your inquiries and schedule consultations.
  • Send you transactional SMS and email messages related to your booking — such as scheduling outreach, confirmations, reminders, and follow-ups.
  • Deliver automation services you engage us for.
  • Improve our website, chat assistant, and automation offerings.
  • Comply with legal and accounting obligations.

4. SMS and text messaging

When you provide your phone number through our chat widget, voice booking call, or in reply to our business number, you consent to receive transactional and conversational SMS from Broadhead Automations about your inquiry. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply through your carrier. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP, or get help by replying HELP.

Text messaging originator opt-in data and consent will not be shared with any third parties for marketing purposes.

5. How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only with the third-party service providers we rely on to run our business, and only to the extent needed for them to perform their role:

  • Twilio — SMS and voice telephony.
  • Vapi — AI voice assistant for inbound booking calls.
  • n8n — workflow automation platform orchestrating our chat and booking flows.
  • Google Workspace — email and calendar for scheduling.
  • Anthropic — the large language model powering our chat assistant.

Each of these providers handles data under their own privacy and security terms. We may also share information when required by law, to enforce our rights, or to protect the safety of users or the public.

6. Data retention

We keep personal information only as long as needed to provide our services, maintain business records, and comply with legal obligations. You can request deletion at any time by emailing us.

7. Your rights

You may request to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correct information that is inaccurate or out of date.
  • Delete your personal information, subject to any legal obligations we have to retain it.
  • Opt out of SMS by replying STOP; opt out of email by replying to any message or contacting us.

To make a request, email sean@broadheadautomations.com. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

8. Children

Our services are intended for use by adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

9. Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect the information we collect. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we take the protection of your information seriously.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of our services after changes are posted means you accept the updated policy.

11. Contact

Questions about this policy or your data? Email sean@broadheadautomations.com.
